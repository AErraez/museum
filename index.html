The Metropolitan Museum of Art (MET) is one of the world's most renowned art museums, located in the Manhattan district of New York City, United States. The museum's collection includes more than two million works of art from around the world.

We invite you to explore this portal, where you can discover the wide variety of artistic pieces housed in the museum. We hope you enjoy this experience and feel inspired to visit the museum on one of your future trips.

How to Use This Portal

To navigate this portal, start by selecting the time period, country of origin and object type that interest you. Based on your selection, a gallery of images will appear at the bottom of the page, showcasing artworks that match your chosen filters.
